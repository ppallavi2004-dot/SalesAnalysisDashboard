#SalesAnalysisDashboard
##Overview
The SalesAnalysisDashboard is a Power BI project built to analyze sales performance across products, categories, and channels. It combines transactional and master data to highlight trends, profitability, and customer behavior.
##Features
•	Visualizes daily and monthly sales trends.
•	Compares Direct Sales, Online, and Wholesaler channels.
•	Tracks payment modes (Cash vs. Online).
•	Highlights top selling and margin rich products.
•	Provides category wise insights (Grocery, Household, Personal Care, Beverages).
##Data Sources
•	Sales-Dashboard-Data.xlsx – Transactional and product master data.
•	SalesAnalysisDashboard.pbix – Power BI dashboard file.
## Insights
•	Direct Sales dominate overall transactions.
•	Online sales show strong growth momentum.
•	Margin rich products include Silk Fabric, Carpet Roll, and Wheat Flour.
•	Essentials like Salt and Match Box remain low margin but high volume.
